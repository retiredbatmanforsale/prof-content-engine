# Interactive Curriculum Tree

Welcome to the **AI for Leaders Interactive Curriculum Tree** - your personalized roadmap through the complete AI for Leaders program. This interactive visualization replaces traditional flat curriculum lists with an engaging, gamified learning experience designed specifically for business leaders.

## What You'll Experience

### 🎯 **Personalized Learning Paths**
Choose your role and see a customized curriculum that highlights the topics most relevant to your position:
- **C-Suite Executives**: Strategic focus on governance and competitive advantage
- **Product Leaders**: AI integration into products and business strategy  
- **Engineering Leaders**: Technical depth and implementation expertise
- **Business Unit Leaders**: Operational impact and change management

### 🌳 **Visual Learning Progression**
- **4 Clear Phases**: Foundation → Technical Depth → Business Applications → Strategic Implementation
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual indicators show your completion status
- **Interactive Exploration**: Click to expand phases and explore topics
- **Business Context**: Each topic shows its business value and strategic importance

### 🏆 **Gamification Elements**
- **Achievement Tracking**: Monitor your progress across all phases
- **Status Indicators**: Clear visual feedback on completed, in-progress, and planned content
- **Progress Persistence**: Your progress is saved and remembered across sessions
- **Motivational Design**: Engaging visuals that encourage continued learning

## How to Use This Tree

### **1. Select Your Learning Path**
Choose your role from the dropdown to see your personalized curriculum. The tree will highlight the phases and topics most relevant to your position.

### **2. Explore Phases and Topics**
- **Click phase headers** to expand and see all topics within that phase
- **Hover over topics** to see detailed descriptions and business value
- **Click topics** to navigate directly to the content or external tools

### **3. Track Your Progress**
- **Visual indicators** show completion status for each topic
- **Progress bars** display completion percentage for each phase
- **Achievement summary** tracks your overall progress

### **4. Navigate Seamlessly**
- **Internal links** take you directly to AI for Leaders content
- **External links** connect to interactive tools like our EDA webapp
- **Smart routing** ensures you always know where you are in your learning journey

## Getting Started

### **New to AI for Leaders?**
Start with **Phase 1: Foundation** to build your AI fundamentals. The Machine Learning Fundamentals topic provides the essential foundation for everything that follows.

### **Already Have AI Experience?**
Use the role selector to find your personalized path. Engineering Leaders might jump to Phase 2 for technical depth, while C-Suite Executives might focus on Phase 4 for strategic implementation.

### **Want Hands-On Experience?**
Look for topics with external links, especially our [EDA Web Application](https://eda.aiseekhegaindia.com/) for hands-on data exploration and analysis.

## Learning Philosophy

This curriculum is designed with **business leaders in mind**:

- **Strategic Focus**: Every topic connects to real business value and competitive advantage
- **Progressive Complexity**: Start with intuitive concepts and gradually introduce technical depth
- **Market-Driven**: Real-world examples and industry applications throughout
- **Leader-Focused**: Emphasizes decision-making and strategic implications over technical implementation

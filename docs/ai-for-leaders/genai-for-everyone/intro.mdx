# AI 4 Everyone: Gen AI Systems, LLMs, Prompting & Applied AI Use cases

This course explains **generative AI**, **LLMs**, **prompting**, and **applied use cases** in plain language—so leaders can adopt tools safely, ask better questions of vendors, and align teams on what is real versus hype.

Work through the lessons **in order**; each one adds vocabulary you will reuse in pilots and reviews.

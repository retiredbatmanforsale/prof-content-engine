# Attention Is All You Need

This course walks through the **transformer** architecture: why it replaced **RNNs/LSTMs** for many sequence tasks, how **token and positional embeddings** feed **attention**, and how **encoder** and **decoder** stacks combine into the model from [Vaswani et al. (2017)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.03762).

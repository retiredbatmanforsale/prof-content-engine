# My notes: from MIT to agentic IDEs

## What I tell students (including myself)

I still believe in <strong>fundamentals</strong>. I also believe tools are now <strong>table stakes</strong>: if I refuse them, I am not noble—I am slow. The strongest people I meet pair <strong>deep CS thinking</strong> with fluent tool use. I need judgment because models optimize <strong>trade-offs</strong> I never stated—when I say “rewrite module X,” I do not mean “rewrite the universe,” unless I am careless.

For research, I am skeptical that models replace <em>core</em> discovery tomorrow—but I already see them eating boilerplate, and I plan to use that time wisely.

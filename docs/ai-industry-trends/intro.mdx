# AI Industry Trends

This track surfaces **first-hand perspectives from industry**: how AI products evolve, how teams measure progress, and how roles—from research to engineering—are shifting as models and tooling improve.

Lessons are structured for **quick scanning** (timelines, tables, themes) while preserving the **substance** of each session.
